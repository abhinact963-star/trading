Candlestick Basics

Every chart is made of candles. Once you understand one candle, you understand all of them.

What Does a Candle Show You?

Each candle records four things for that time period. Open — where price started. High — the highest it went. Low — the lowest it went. Close — where it ended. The fat part in the middle is the body. The thin lines above and below are called wicks. The wick shows how far price went before getting pulled back.

Green Candle vs. Red Candle

Green candle (teal on this site) = price went UP during that period. Buyers won. Red candle (coral) = price went DOWN. Sellers won. That's it. Every candle is a tiny battle between buyers and sellers. The colour tells you who won.

Why Candles Are Better Than Line Charts

A normal line chart only shows you where price ended. It hides everything else. A candle shows you the whole story: how far buyers pushed up, how far sellers pushed down, and who was in control when time ran out. More information = better decisions. That's why traders worldwide use candlestick charts.
