Volume Analysis & Volume Profile

Price tells you where; volume tells you how convicted the move was.

Volume Basics & Confirmation

A move on rising volume is generally more reliable than the same move on shrinking volume — volume is read as a proxy for participation and conviction.

Animation — Price with volume bars (strong vs weak moves)

Climactic Volume & Divergence

A sudden volume spike far above average often marks exhaustion. Volume divergence — price making a new high/low while volume shrinks — can foreshadow a reversal.

Volume Profile, POC, and the Value Area

Volume Profile plots volume against price, showing which levels traded the most. POC = price with most volume. Value Area = ~70% of total volume. VAH/VAL = boundaries.
