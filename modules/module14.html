Trading Psychology

The mechanism by which a sound strategy gets undone by the person executing it.

Fear & Greed

Fear causes premature exits and hesitation to take valid setups. Greed causes oversized positions and holding past a planned exit.

FOMO & Overtrading

Fear of missing out drives entries with no real setup. Overtrading — far more trades than the strategy calls for — usually follows.

Revenge Trading

Trying to immediately "win back" a loss with a larger, less disciplined trade — one of the most reliable ways to turn one loss into an account-ending sequence.

Animation — Revenge trading cycle: loss spiraling into larger losses

Discipline & Journaling

Keeping a written trade journal — entry reason, outcome, what was followed or broken — is one of the few habits with strong evidence of improving performance over time.
